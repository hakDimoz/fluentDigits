FluentDigits

What is FluentDigits?

How to use

  1. Choose your target language and the range of numbers you want to practise.
  2. A random number will be generated and played in your chosen language.
  3. Type what you think the number is.
  4. Try to build a streak of correct answers!

Hello everyone!
As a language learner, I've always found it difficult to remember and comprehend numbers in a foreign language. So, I decided to make this little web app to help myself practise and to improve my programming skills too...
